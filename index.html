VIRTOUSI - The Music Society of IIIT Allahabad



Virtuosi- The music club of IIIT Allahabad stands to be one of the most entertaining and talented clubs. We at virtuosi believe in innovation. We strongly encourage interest in music. We hold many events all across the year. We are strong contributors to entertainment in the annual cultural event Effervescence. The club has three coordinators who look after the smooth functioning of the club. And further we have senior members who help out the coordinators in making the club more authoritative.

The club has rigourous interviews to become members of the club. The members have the duty to get work done and report to the coordinators if any problem arises. The club is quite transparent in its functioning.
